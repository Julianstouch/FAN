My first AngularJS expression



Your email in uppercase is: {{myEmail | uppercase}}

Notes:

  • input is a directive that optionally needs ng-model.
  • ng-model synchronizes the input content with $rootScope.myEmail.
  • An expression put a watcher.
  • This is the watcher that evaluates and write the expression evaluation in the view.
